Technology and Information System

Technology refers to the application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes, encompassing tools, machines, systems, and processes that enhance human capabilities and efficiency. It includes everything from software and hardware to communication systems and innovative practices. Information System is a structured system for collecting, storing, processing, and disseminating information. It combines technology, people, and processes to manage data and support decision-making, typically involving databases, software applications, and communication networks. Information systems help organizations streamline operations, improve communication, and enhance overall efficiency.
